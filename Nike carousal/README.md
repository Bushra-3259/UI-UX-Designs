Clicking on a colored dot reveals its respective Nike shoe.  
- If the selected shoe is the **next** in sequence, the current shoe slides out and the next shoe slides in from the opposite side.  
- If the selected shoe is the **previous**, the current shoe slides out and the previous shoe enters from the opposite side.
